About me

Alright, so my name is Madelen Sletteberg, but you can call me Maddie. I come equipped with two bachelor's degrees in linguistics and nursing, both of which I'm not using — because, well, life's too short to stick with Plan A when you haven't found your true passion yet. It's important to me to spend my life doing something that brings me joy and peace of mind.

I thrive on creativity and problem-solving, and there's something incredibly satisfying about how code can bring order to chaos. Now, I'm not exactly a designer — I don't have a natural eye for aesthetics — but I absolutely love the challenge of turning a designer's vision into a functional reality through code.

When I'm not buried in code, I'm likely scaling a rock wall, mountain biking, or exploring the great outdoors with a camping or packrafting trip. I've recently taken up sewing my own clothes (sustainable living for the win!), and I'm a voracious reader. My dream job? Writing fiction. But until I hit the bestseller lists, I'm happily combining my love for storytelling with front-end development.

Why MaddiPaddi?

When I was younger, people would call me "MaddePadde" (with "Padde" meaning "toad"). As a kid, I wasn't thrilled about the toad association, as they can be quite unappealing. But as I grew up and matured, I decided to embrace the nickname. Thus, "MaddiPaddi" was born, becoming my go-to nickname and gamertag for every account I created.

Now I really like it, and it inspired my logo "TechToad" and the design for this site. Because who wouldn't want a toad with a tech twist as their personal mascot?
